About me

Born and raised in Racine, Wisconsin, I started my life journey with an enlistment to the United States Air Force. As an Avionics/Radar Technician, I realized my passion for troubleshooting and technology. After 6 years, I decided to work for the Federal Government at the Robins AFB Air Logistics Center.

I received several medals and accomendations during my tenure while working for the U.S. Government and I'm proud to say I'm an Air Force Veteran. I enjoy video games, researching new technologies and most of all, being a proud parent to my THREE beautiful children.

I love to challenge myself and others, and enjoy working on a team. I'm a firm believer in "no one left behind", and I implement time and risk management processes in all I do from coding to parenting.
